About Fractional IT

Our Story

Fractional IT was founded with a vision to provide top-tier IT and cybersecurity solutions to businesses in Missoula and beyond. We understand the unique challenges faced by companies in our mountain community and are committed to delivering tailored, cutting-edge technology services that drive growth and ensure security.

Andy Morse - Founder

With over 20 years of experience in the IT and Cybersecurity fields, Andy Morse brings a wealth of knowledge and expertise to Fractional IT. His passion for technology and commitment to excellence have been the driving forces behind our company's success.

Andy's extensive background spans various aspects of information technology and security, allowing him to provide comprehensive solutions that address the complex needs of modern businesses. His forward-thinking approach and deep understanding of the evolving tech landscape ensure that our clients always stay ahead of the curve.
