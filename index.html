Andy Morse

Andy Morse founded Fractional IT on a belief he's held for over two decades in the technology industry: every business — regardless of size — deserves enterprise-grade IT and security. Not just the companies with deep pockets and large IT departments. Every business.

With 20+ years of hands-on experience spanning IT infrastructure, cybersecurity, and strategic technology leadership, Andy has seen firsthand how the right technology partner transforms a business. He built Fractional IT to deliver that same expertise at a price and model that works for real companies — the ones that actually run the economy.
